# Senior Backend Engineer — Stipendl

**Company:** Stipendl is a fictional Singapore-based fintech building cross-border payroll and contractor payouts for SE Asia. We move money in 14 currencies for ~3,000 employers and we're scaling fast.

**Role:** Senior Backend Engineer on the Payments Platform team. You'll own services that handle settlement, FX, and reconciliation. Tight feedback loop with compliance and ops.

**What you'll do**
- Design and ship payment services (Go or TypeScript) on AWS.
- Own a domain end-to-end: schema, API, deploy pipeline, on-call.
- Drive reconciliation and ledgering correctness across providers (Wise, Airwallex, local banks).
- Mentor 1–2 mid-level engineers.

## Must-haves

1. 6+ years building production backend services at scale.
2. Strong in Go *or* TypeScript/Node, plus PostgreSQL.
3. Direct experience with payments, ledgering, or financial systems (anywhere in the stack).
4. Comfortable owning on-call rotations for revenue-critical services.

## Nice-to-haves

1. Prior fintech / regulated environment experience.
2. Familiarity with double-entry accounting concepts.
3. Exposure to South-East Asian payment rails (FAST, PayNow, DuitNow, PromptPay).
4. Track record of mentoring or tech-lead work.

We're hybrid (3 days in Singapore office). Visa sponsorship available for the right candidate.
