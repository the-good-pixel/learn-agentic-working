# Stack — B2B Sales Team

Typical stack for a six-person outbound-and-inbound sales team at a mid-market B2B SaaS.

- **HubSpot** — CRM, deal pipeline, sequences.
- **Apollo** — prospect data, list-building, outbound sequences.
- **Gong** — call recording, deal-room intelligence.
- **Slack** — team comms, #wins channel, deal-desk threads.
- **Google Workspace** — Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Sheets.
- **Loom** — async video walkthroughs to prospects.
- **ZoomInfo** — enrichment and intent signals.
