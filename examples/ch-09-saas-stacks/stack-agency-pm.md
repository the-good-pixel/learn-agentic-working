# Stack — Agency Project Manager

Typical stack for a PM at a 20-person creative / digital agency juggling 4–6 client projects.

- **Asana** — per-client project boards, task assignment, deadlines.
- **Toggl** — time tracking against client codes for billing.
- **Slack** — internal channels per client; some clients have shared channels.
- **Google Drive** — deliverables, shared client folders.
- **QuickBooks** — invoicing clients, vendor payments.
- **Mailchimp** — newsletter sends on behalf of clients.
- **Figma** — review and comment on design work in flight.
