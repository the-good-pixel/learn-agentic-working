# Stack — Solo Startup Founder

Typical SaaS stack for a solo or two-person early-stage software company.

- **Stripe** — payments, subscription billing, customer portal.
- **Linear** — issue tracking, roadmap, cycle planning.
- **Notion** — docs, wiki, sales playbook, onboarding notes.
- **Resend** — transactional and marketing email from the product.
- **Vercel** — hosting and deploys for the marketing site and the app.
- **Cal.com** — booking link for sales calls and customer demos.
- **Pylon** — shared inbox and customer support across email + Slack-Connect.
