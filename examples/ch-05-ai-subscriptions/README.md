# Ch. 5 — AI Subscriptions Audit Exercise

A fictional one-month company card statement (PDF, 2 pages, ~85 line items) so you can practice the AI-subscription audit without exposing your real one.

## Exercise

> Read `card-statement.pdf`. List every line item that has "AI" in its name or is an AI add-on bundled into an underlying tool. For each one, draft a one-line memo: what I'd use it for, and could my agent do that job against the underlying system instead. Rank from "most replaceable" to "genuinely needed".

Look for the planted teaching moment: `PROOFINGAI` at $29/mo sits alongside `GRAMMARFIX BASIC` at $5/mo — the AI upcharge is **more expensive than the underlying tool**. There's a similar trap with `NOTIQ BASE PLAN` at $8 versus `NOTIQ AI ADD-ON` at $24 for 3 seats. A good audit catches both.

The non-AI items (Adobio, Figmary, Streamhost, office supplies, travel) are there to make the signal real — the audit is about separating AI subs from everything else, not flagging every line.
