# Ch. 5 — AI Subscriptions Audit Exercise

A fictional one-month company card statement so you can practice the AI-subscription audit without exposing your real one.

## Exercise

> Read `card-statement.txt`. List every line item that has "AI" in its name or is an AI add-on bundled into an underlying tool. For each one, draft a one-line memo: what I'd use it for, and could my agent do that job against the underlying system instead. Rank from "most replaceable" to "genuinely needed".

Look for the planted teaching moment: `PROOFINGAI GRAMMAR PRO` at $29/mo replaces a $5/mo standard plan — the AI upcharge is **more expensive than the underlying tool**. A good audit catches that.

The non-AI items (Adobio, Figmary, Streamhost, office supplies, travel) are there to make the signal real — the audit is about separating AI subs from everything else, not flagging every line.
