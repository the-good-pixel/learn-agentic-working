# Ch. 14 — Parallel Sub-agents Exercise

A ready-made three-angle research brief for the "fan out, fan in" exercise. Acme Foods and the CEO are fictional; the structure of the question is real.

## Exercise

> Read `research-brief.md`. Spawn three sub-agents — one per sub-question — and let them research in parallel. Have your main agent synthesize when they return.

Compare wall-clock time vs. running the three in series. The synthesized output should be grounded in concrete findings from each sub-agent (with specifics you didn't supply), and total time should be close to the slowest of the three — not the sum.
